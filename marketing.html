AdMania will be a brand new event of OJAS 7.0 which will test the advertising acumen of participants. Idea is to link the competition to the theme of the Mahabharata but still keep it fun and interesting. To do this we have three proposals.

  1. Create an ad for a product/service using a character from the Mahabharata as the brand ambassador. Eg: Bheema being part of an ad for gym equipment/ the Kauravas being part of an ad for birth control.
  2. Another idea was to have two rounds wherein the first round would involve taking a contemporary product like say an Ipod, back to the Mahabharata age and designing an advertising campaign to sell the same to the consumers of that era. The next round could involve getting a product from the Mahabharata era and designing an advertisement to sell it to the modern day consumer.
  3. An ad campaign for the promotion of any key event in the course of Mahabharata. Eg: Draupadi ka Swayamvar, a fictitious Game of Dice tournament, a call for warriors before the start of Mahabharata.
The competition will have 2 rounds.
  1. Round 1: Online submission of entries. Deliverables will be
    • Long Copy Ad/Poster
    • 1 page concept note explaining the idea behind the ad, target group, etc.
    The AdMania facebook page will post each entry and there will be a 30% weightage for number of likes for the respective posts.
  2. Round 2: Based on the entries, we will shortlist 6 teams for on campus round. The participants will have to come prepared with the following deliverables:
    • 60 sec Video Ad
    • PowerPoint Presentation (explaining the idea behind the video ad)
    • 20 sec Radio Ad (Maybe on the spot with an hour to prepare depending on logistical constraints)
    The video will be judged out of 30 marks, PPT out of 10, the radio ad out of 10 giving a consolidated score of 50. The team with the highest consolidated score will be declared the winner.

Explore the concepts of branding, test your mettle and rack your imagination to discover the true marketer in you.

As the flagship event of Marketing at Ojas 7.0, the management festival of SPJIMR, Brand-O looks to discover the best marketers across B-schools of the country by measuring the innate marketing sense and branding ability in each one of you.

The event comprises of three rounds.
  1. Round 1 (Online):
    2. The first round will be an online quiz comprising of a set of questions on Marketing and Brand Management. This will be an elimination round and top 20 teams will be selected.
  2. Round 2 (Off-Campus):
    3. Round 2 will be a branding challenge, where teams would be asked to submit a presentation about branding an idea. The idea can be anything under the sun. The idea would be released by organizing team as per the schedule. Participants would be expected to provide innovative and feasible description of their branding idea.
  3. Round 3 (On-Campus):
    4. Round 3 will be an on-campus round where the 6 finalist teams will be given a case study on brand management. The teams will be asked to present their solutions to a panel of jury members.
