As the curtains raise this Ojas, and the audience waits with bated breath, it’s time to leave all your inhibitions behind and arouse the actor within you. The lights have dimmed, the mood is set and the stage is ready. So get ready to put on your acting caps and transform ingeniously written scripts into mesmerizing plays. 'Act Zone' sets the perfect platform for these actors to catch the spotlight with drama, humour and zeal, and enthrall the ever enthusiastic audience of Ojas 7.0.

The event comprises two rounds
Round 1:
• Teams have to register for the event via the Ojas website.
• Teams then have to make a theatrical trailer which should include clips and/or pictures from their play. Teams are free to use graphics, background scores etc to make the trailer. It should have a minimum duration of 1 minute and a maximum duration of 3 minutes.
• Steps for submission of the video trailer -
1.The video needs to be uploaded on you tube.
2.The you tube video link has to be posted on the Act Zone Event page on Facebook with the caption as <"TeamName_InstituteName">.
• Registered teams will be sent the details of Facebook events page of Act Zone. They are required to 'Attend' the event.
• The trailer has to be uploaded on the SPJIMR Ojas Facebook page. The number of likes received for the video on YouTube will be a component in the short listing process.
• The last day for submission of entries is 11th January, 2014.
Round 2 :
Teams will have to perform on campus in front of a panel of judges. Each team can perform for a maximum of 15 minutes.
    •A team can compose of a maximum of 10 persons with one optional musician (no electronic instruments allowed). The members of a team cannot be from different institutes.
    • One participant cannot be part of more than one team.
    • No change in the team structure is allowed after the team is registered. •Props if needed have to be brought by the participants.
    • Not adhering to the time limits would lead to reduction in points. Vulgarity would lead to reduction in points. Use of abusive language is prohibited
    •The decisions of the organizers of the contest and the panel of judges will be final and
    •binding on all the participants. The Terms and Conditions of participation are subject to change at any time without specific notice. The amended Terms and Conditions of entry will be effective immediately upon being posted on the website. It is the sole responsibility of the teams to regularly visit the Website to read any changesfor a maximum of 15 minutes.
Last Date for uploading Trailer Videos: 11th January, 2015 (11:00 PM).
First Round Results: 13th January, 2015 (6:00 PM).
Finals: To be announcedresponsibility of the teams to regularly visit the Website to read any changesfor a maximum of 15 minutes.
